Are you stuck planetside with those boring old terrestrial games? Hop into the cockpit with Mister Quasar's Courier Service!

Test your mettle against a slew of classic arcade genres, updated for the future. That's right! It's a "retro" "sci-fi" "indie" "game"!

And it's up to you to help the crew of the Miss Lonelyhearts stay alive (and maybe even stay airborne).
